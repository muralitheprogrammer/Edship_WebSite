◆ Links To Other Sites

When you install the Edship mobile app, certain permissions are requested by default. As these are not customizable during installation, below is a summary of permissions and their intended use: Location: To track your current location for features like Track My Bus. Camera & Photos: Used to capture or upload images for assignments, homework, or profile updates. Notifications: To keep you informed about updates like assignments, circulars, results, news, and more. Storage & Files: Required to upload and download files, documents, or multimedia from your device. Contacts (Android only): Helps simplify OTP entry by detecting the verification number from your contact list (only with user permission). File Access: Allows interaction with external storage for saving and retrieving relevant documents or images.

◆ Android Permissions:

ocation: This permission is used to locate your current location in the Track My Bus feature. So, you can know where you are and where the bus is reached. Photos: Save photos from Gallery to the device's camera roll. Camera: Use your camera to take photos in Create Homework and Create Assignment. Notifications: You will receive notifications for Circulars, Assignments, Queries, Query Queues, Blog Posts, News, Results, Result Sheets, Gallery, Attendance, Polls, Activities, etc. Storage: This permission is required to upload documents/photos from external storage and to save documents/photos to external storage. Contact List: Users can quickly choose phone numbers to receive OTPs by granting access to their contact list, which helps prevent fraudulent behavior. Image: Save images from the gallery to the camera roll and use the camera to take visuals and upload them to the app. File Information: This permission is required to upload files or images from external storage and to save them.

◆ iOS Permissions (EdShip):

Location: This permission allows EdShip to access your location for features such as “Track My Bus” so users can know where the bus is and its estimated arrival time. Photos: Enables EdShip to save photos from the device’s gallery to the camera roll. Camera: Allows EdShip to take photos for use in modules like Homework Submission or Assignment Creation. Notifications: You will receive alerts for features such as Circulars, Assignments, Queries, Queues, News, Results, Attendance, Polls, and Activities. Storage: Required for uploading/downloading documents and photos to/from the device. Contact List: This helps users autofill phone numbers during OTP verification, reducing fraud. Image: Saves gallery images to the camera roll and enables camera access to capture/upload visuals. File Information: Allows EdShip to upload and save files or images from your device.

EdShip does not permit general end-users (such as students) to create personal accounts directly.
